FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Do you offer free estimates?

For many jobs we can give a ballpark estimate from photos and details. For complex issues, we’ll schedule an on‑site diagnostic and apply that cost toward the repair when possible.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We can provide proof of insurance and licensing details on request.

What payment types do you accept?

Cash, major cards, and digital payments. Invoices provided for all work.

Can you work in condos and older buildings?

Absolutely. We’re used to Boston’s older plumbing, tight access, and building requirements. We coordinate with property managers when needed.
